behold that tree in autumns dim decay stripped by the frequent chill and eddying wind where yet some yellow lonely leaves we find lingering and trembling on the naked spray twenty perchance for millions whirled away emblem also too just of humankind vain man expects longevity designed for few indeed and their protracted day what is it worth that wisdom does not scorn the blasts of sickness care and grief appal that laid the friends in dust whose natal morn rose near their own and solemn is the call yet like those weak deserted leaves forlorn shivering they cling to life and fear to fall
